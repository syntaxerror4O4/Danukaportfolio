Memento - Momente

Interactive Poster with Face Recognition

A moment can last forever or only seconds what will you grasp?

MOMENTE a very small space of time
MEMENTO something that serves to keep alive the memory of a person or event

Simple Interactions lead to change which are only graspable for the duration of your own standtsill. Words become visible written in a digital altered version of my handwriting.
