Cyclops

Robot // 3d printed robot case, PlatformIO, ESP32 DevKit board, photoresistors,
adafruit neopixel ring (20LEDs) 2x servo motors

A playful approach creating a robot that can recharge your phone yet gets angry as soon as possible you withdraw too much energy. Through this project, we want to question the amount of energy we waste while overcharging technical devices.

ZHdK, Physical computing, 2019, Empathetic machines

Students: Danuka Ana Tomas (IAD), Tim Fuchs (IAD)
Lecturers: Luke Franzke (IAD), Florian Bruggisser (IAD)
