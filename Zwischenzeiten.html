Zwischen Zeiten

Interactive Installation, Katholische Kirche Witikon, Zürich (ZH)

We seek change in motion, between moments, between experiences, between times.

Interactive installation that deals with the feeling of community and the sensation
of space and time. Visitors can actively shape the effect of the space
by walking through it and thereby letting balloons automatically rise and fall.
